# Product Requirement Document (PRD)
## Feature: AI Math Grader

---

### 1. Overview
This feature introduces AI-powered grading specifically tailored for **Mathematics** student answers. It is intended to handle symbolic equations, step-by-step logic, numeric precision, and unit awareness according to a predefined marking logic.

### 2. Status
🚧 **Under initial R&D Phase.** Full details, flow diagrams, and marking constraints (e.g., partial marks for correct formulas vs. wrong final answers) to be defined in subsequent development cycles.
