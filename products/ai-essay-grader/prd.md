# Product Requirement Document (PRD)
## Feature: AI Essay Grader

---

### 1. Overview
This feature introduces AI-powered grading specifically tailored for **Essay** student answers. It is intended to handle unstructured, long-form text responses and evaluate them based on semantic meaning compared to a marking scheme.

### 2. Status
🚧 **Under initial R&D Phase.** Full details, flow diagrams, and marking logic to be defined in subsequent development cycles.
